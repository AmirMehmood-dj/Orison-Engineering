Our Product Categories

Orison Engineering Services specializes in supplying high-quality electrical automation products from leading manufacturers. Explore our comprehensive range of industrial automation components designed for reliability and performance.

PLCs & HMI Systems

Premium programmable logic controllers and human-machine interfaces from top brands. Perfect for automated control systems, process monitoring, and industrial applications with reliable performance.

Control Panel Components

Complete range of electrical panel components including contactors, relays, circuit breakers, and switches. All products meet international standards and offer superior durability.

Industrial Sensors

Comprehensive selection of proximity sensors, photoelectric sensors, temperature sensors, and pressure sensors for precise detection and measurement in industrial environments.

Power & Energy Products

Energy monitoring devices, power meters, transformers, and electrical protection equipment. Optimize your energy consumption and ensure safe electrical operations.

Electric Motors

High-performance electric motors designed for industrial applications. Ensure reliable operation, energy efficiency, and long-lasting performance across a range of use cases.

Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)

Advanced VFD systems to control motor speed and torque efficiently. Reduce energy consumption, enhance motor lifespan, and improve process control in automation systems.
